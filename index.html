Saravanaa Bhavan, previously Hotel Saravana Bhavan, is an Indian restaurant chain based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The chain serves South Indian cuisine and operates 33 locations in India (24 in Chennai) and 92+ across 28 countries in North America, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and Oceania.Currently, Saravanaa Bhavan has multiple overseas locations which are run by franchisees. .
